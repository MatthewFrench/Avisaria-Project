Dieses carrier Spriteset ist das Kernstück des carriers  . Die Animationen dieses Spritepaketes sind mit denen der anderen verknüpft. Zum Beispiel mit denen des carrier bars ...

Attention :

This carrier Spriteset is the mainpiece of the carrier package.  The animations of this Spriteset are connected to the animations of the carrier . Like the one of carrier bars ...

Because there wasn´t enough space to display some of the Animations at an 96 X 96 Tile, i have rendered them in a size of 128 x 128.
This means that the zero point of this animations is -16 /-16 compared to the rest of the sprite ...
That´s why they are in an extrafolder.
